Alternative Rock Songs You Should Care About

WHY?

Alternative rock/punk is one of the more underrated and neglected genres of music. Yet, alternative rock offers many unique qualities that set it apart from the mainstream. Emotional depth, social and cultural commentary, and qualities of escapism are a few features of alternative rock music that make it a more enriching and fulfilling listening experience. Whether it's feelings of love, loss, angst, or existentialism, alternative rock is sure to make you feel "some typa way". Here are a couple song recommendations, complete with artist and album title, in hopes of successfully turning you into an alternative rock stan."
